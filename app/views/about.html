About LUDO

Welcome to the ultimate multiplayer LUDO game! Here is how you can play and enjoy with friends and family:

  • Starting the Game: Each player rolls the dice. The highest roller begins, and play moves clockwise.
  • Moving Pieces: Roll the dice and move any one of your pieces forward by the number shown on the dice.
  • Safety Squares: Landing on a colored square means you're safe from being sent back home by other players.
  • Goal: The first player to move all four pieces into the home triangle wins.

This game is a great way to develop strategy and probability skills, as well as learning how to cooperate and compete healthily in a fun environment!
